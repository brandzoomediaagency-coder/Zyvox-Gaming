About ZyvoxGaming

ZyvoxGaming is an independent gaming-gear review and recommendation site built for Indian gamers. We help you cut through the marketing and find the best gaming mice, keyboards, headsets, monitors, GPUs and more — with honest reviews, side-by-side comparisons and clear buying guides.

We're a review site — you buy on Amazon

Important: ZyvoxGaming does not sell products or take payments. We review gaming gear and link you to Amazon, where you complete your purchase securely with Amazon's own checkout and buyer protection. You never pay us anything.

What we do

Every recommendation is based on manufacturer specifications, expert consensus, and aggregated owner reviews. We focus on the metrics that actually affect gameplay — sensor accuracy, weight, switch feel, refresh rate, comfort, battery life and value — rather than marketing claims or headline numbers like peak DPI.

How we review

  • ▸Performance first. We judge gear on what matters in real use, not spec-sheet bragging rights.
  • ▸Value for India. We factor in price and availability on Amazon India, and flag when sales are the best time to buy.
  • ▸Honest trade-offs. Every review lists the cons as clearly as the pros.
  • ▸Editorial independence. We earn a commission when you buy through our links, but that never changes our rankings or verdicts.
