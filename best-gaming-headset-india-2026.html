How to choose a gaming headset in India (2026)

Wired vs wireless

Wired headsets are cheaper and never need charging; modern 2.4 GHz wireless is lag-free and more convenient if your budget allows.

Microphone quality

If you play as a team or stream, look for a clear detachable or flip-to-mute mic.

Comfort for long sessions

Memory-foam cushions and a lighter frame keep you comfortable over hours of play.

Frequently asked questions

Is a wired or wireless gaming headset better?▾

Wired is cheaper and never needs charging; modern 2.4 GHz wireless is lag-free and more convenient. Choose by budget and comfort.

Is surround sound worth it for gaming?▾

Virtual surround can help you place footsteps in FPS games, but clear stereo drivers matter more.

How much should I spend on a gaming headset in India?▾

Great budget wired headsets are cheap, mid-range wired ones add comfort and mic quality, and wireless sits at the higher end.
