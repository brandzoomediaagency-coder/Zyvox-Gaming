How to choose a gaming headset in India (2026 buying guide)

Wired or wireless?

Wired headsets are cheaper and never need charging — best value on a budget. Modern 2.4 GHz wireless (like the Arctis Nova 7) is effectively lag-free and far more convenient if you can spend more.

How important is the microphone?

If you play as a team or stream, a clear mic matters. Detachable or retractable mics (Cloud II, Nova 7) sound cleaner and tuck away when not in use.

Is surround sound worth it?

Virtual surround can help you place footsteps in FPS games, but clear, well-tuned stereo drivers matter more. Prioritise a good headset over a big surround number.

What about comfort for long sessions?

Look for memory-foam cushions and a light frame. Lighter headsets like the G435 and well-padded ones like the Kraken V3 stay comfortable over hours of play.
