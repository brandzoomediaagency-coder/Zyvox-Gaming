How to choose a gaming keyboard in India (2026)

Mechanical vs membrane

Mechanical switches give a consistent, tactile and durable keypress most gamers prefer. Good budget membrane boards are still fine to start.

Which switch — blue, brown or red

Red switches are smooth and fast for gaming, brown are tactile but quieter, and blue are clicky and loud.

TKL, 60% or full-size

TKL and 60% layouts free up desk space for low-sensitivity aiming; full-size keeps the numpad for work.

Frequently asked questions

Is a mechanical keyboard better for gaming?▾

Generally yes — mechanical switches are more consistent, tactile and durable than membrane keyboards.

What is the difference between blue, brown and red switches?▾

Blue are clicky/loud, brown are tactile/quiet, and red are smooth linear switches favoured for fast gaming.

How much should I spend on a gaming keyboard in India?▾

Solid budget mechanical boards start around ₹2,500, with premium wireless boards costing more. Sales bring big discounts.
