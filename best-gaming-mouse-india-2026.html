How to choose a gaming mouse in India (2026 buying guide)

Does DPI actually matter?

Not as much as the box suggests. Most competitive players use just 400–1600 DPI. A huge maximum DPI is marketing — what matters is a sensor that tracks accurately with no acceleration, smoothing or jitter across your mousepad.

How much does weight matter for FPS?

A lot, if you play fast shooters. Lighter mice (under ~80g) are easier to flick and cause less fatigue. If you prefer slower, deliberate aim or larger hands, a heavier mouse like the G502 HERO can feel more controlled.

Wired or wireless in 2026?

Modern 2.4 GHz wireless is effectively lag-free and used at the top level of esports, so pick wireless for convenience if your budget allows. At the budget end, wired mice still give you the best sensor for the money.

What grip type should the shape match?

Palm-grip players suit larger ergonomic shapes like the DeathAdder; claw and fingertip players often prefer lighter, shorter mice like the Model O. Match the shape to how you hold the mouse for the best comfort.

When is the best time to buy in India?

Gaming mouse prices drop noticeably during Amazon India sale events. If you're not in a hurry, adding your pick to your wishlist and buying during a sale can save a good chunk — always check the live price before you buy.
