How do we choose the best gaming gear?

Every recommendation on ZyvoxGaming is based on manufacturer specifications, expert consensus, and aggregated owner reviews. We prioritize the metrics that actually affect gameplay — latency, weight, switch durability, refresh rate, and battery life — over marketing claims.

What makes a gaming mouse good in 2026?
Low weight (under 60g), a high-end optical sensor, and high polling rate (1,000–8,000 Hz) for the lowest possible click-to-screen latency.
Is a 240Hz monitor worth it?
For competitive shooters, yes — 240 Hz noticeably improves motion clarity and target tracking versus 144 Hz, provided your GPU can sustain the frame rate.
Wired or wireless for esports?
Modern 2.4 GHz wireless is now competition-legal and effectively latency-free, so choose based on weight and convenience rather than lag.
